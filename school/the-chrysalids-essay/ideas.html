The Chrysalids Essay Ideas

  • what is identity?
    • how one interprets the world
      • influences one's actions and behaviours
      • how the world interprets one's self?
    • influenced by society's views and morals
    • influenced by one's own observations of the environment
    • one's world filter
      • personal bubble
      • protection
      • rose coloured glasses

  • why is identity important?
    • prevents one from being lost in the homogenous sea of faces
    • our lifeline to the world
      • Achilles' heel?
        • held by his mother when dipping him in the Styx
        • prevented Achilles from drowning in the Styx
        • also our one true weakness
    • shapes our relationshipes with others
      • brings happiness?
    • makes the world more interesting
      • old houses vs new houses
        • old houses are built differently from one another
          • more fun to look at
          • requires more work and creativity
        • new houses built mostly the same as one another
          • mind numbing to look at
          • requires less effort to design

  • if your unique identity (ie. exclusive to you, rest of your culture gone, etc) is damaged, there may be no one left to help you repair it
    • see residential schools

  • society doesn't have to accept your identity
    • your identity doesn't have to coincide with society's

  • main points:
    • examining contrast between Waknuk and David
    • importance of developing a personal identity
      • scrutinizing what one is told
      • based on society
        • follow along
        • fully reject
